FEATURES

LIGHT

Not sure how much time your plant spends in the light versus the shade while you're at work? Your bloom device records the levels of light exposed to your plant.

MOISTURE

Overwater your plants and you'll kill them. Underwater your plants and they die too. With Bloom's state of the art sensors, you always know the soil's moisture levels.

TEMPERATURE

Corroborating the temperature with other data on your plants, allows you to draw conclusions on how to best care for your plants. Was it the heat that caused your plant's soil's moisture levels to drop? Or does you plant need to drink more? Now you'll know.

SETUP

PURCHASE

Order a Bloom device and recieve in 1 to 2 weeks.

SET SENSORS

Insert Bloom Moisture Sensor into the soil of your plant, put Light Sensor on leaf or top of plant and place the Temperature Sensor on top of the soil.

REGISTER
