PRODUCT

Bloom uses the CC3200 Micro-controller made by Texas Instruments with sensors to accurately monitor light, moisture, and temperature. Bloom never sleeps and watches over your plant's health 24 hours a day.

LIGHT

The quality, intensity, and duration of light directly impacts a plant's growth. Bloom helps your plant get the optimum light level with accurate information on the amount of light your plant receives with charts.

MOISTURE

A plant cannot grow if it doesnâ€™t have healthy roots, so the proper balance of water is key when growing plants. Bloom continuously monitors the moisture in your plant's soil with state of the art sensors, making sure your plant's roots have the perfect amount of water to grow.

TEMPERATURE

Excessively low or high temperatures may cause: plant stress, inhibit growth, or promote a spindly appearance and foliage damage or drop. Bloom keeps watch of your plant's temperature constantly, informing you of temperature changes that may effect your plant.

FEATURES

USERPROFILE

Get your personal dashboard where you can add multiple Bloom devices, get weather alerts and monitor data regarding your plant's health.

DATA CHARTS

Simple, intuitive charts for your personal computer and mobile device. Get data on the moisture levels, tempetaure and amount of sunlight on your plants.

SETUP

  • POWER DEVICE

    After receiving your device, simply plug your device with the included USB power cable.

  • SETUP SENSORS

    Insert Bloom into the soil near your plant. Make sure that the light sensor at the top of your device is not obstructed to ensure accurate light readings.

  • REGISTER

    Registering your device is simple. Just sign up and register your device. Your device ID is packaged with your Bloom device.

  • CONNECT

    Connect your device to Wi-Fi by entering your Wi-Fi network's information on the keypad on the side of your Bloom device.

  • MONITOR

    Watch the changes in values for moisture, light and temperature in your charts which can be found in your User Profile. Get updates for weather events and get custom text alerts.
